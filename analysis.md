* Incident Analysis Summary

** Key Findings
- Unauthorized access attempts and malware incidents were the most frequent incident types.
- High-severity incidents made up the majority of recorded events.
- VPN Gateway and Endpoint devices were the most commonly affected assets.

** Risk Insights
- Repeated unauthorized access attempts indicate a lack of strong authentication controls.
- Malware incidents suggest gaps in patch management and endpoint protection.
- High severity distribution indicates significant exposure to critical risks.

** Recommendations
- Implement multi-factor authentication (MFA) for remote access systems.
- Strengthen patch management processes for endpoints.
- Conduct regular access reviews and enforce least privilege principles.

** Conclusion
This analysis highlights recurring security issues and demonstrates how structured data can be used to identify trends, prioritize risks, and support decision-making in cybersecurity environments.
